1. Description

SWAPP is a clothing exchange platform that replaces the traditional second-hand buying/selling model with a swapping system. It allows users to refresh their wardrobe frequently, easily, for free, and locally. Thanks to a swipe-based matching system and an interactive map, users can exchange clothes in person, eliminating shipping costs and the pollution associated with deliveries. This human-centered and secure model also allows users to try on items before exchanging them, while fostering strong social connections through the app and through in-person swap events organized several times a month.
